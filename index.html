I am an open source hardware and software enthusiast, passionate about technology, embedded systems and the Web. My interests include a range of topics which could be described as cyber-physical systems. I'm interested by these physically engineered communication and computation cores which have the potential to directly impact our daily lives.

An Electronics and Communication Graduate from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi, I am currently a Graduate student at the Department of Computer and Information Sciences in the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, University of Pennsylvania.
