About

I am an open source hardware and software enthusiast, passionate about technology, embedded systems and the Web. My interests include a range of topics which could be described as cyber-physical systems. I'm interested in development and integration of diverse technology to make solutions which directly impact our daily lives.

An Electronics and Communication Graduate from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi, I am currently a MS student in the Embedded Systems Program (EMBS) at the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, University of Pennsylvania.
