I am currently a final year Electronics and Communication Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) student at Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi, India.

I'm a passionate about technology, embedded systems and the Web. I love learning new technologies, particularly those that allow build robust systems and which give an interative user experience. I am an Open Source hardware-software enthusiast who firmly believes in Free Software, Free Society.

My interests include Embedded System Software and Hardware Designing (FPGAs and Microcontrollers), Compilers, Android and Web Application Development. I am doing my B.E. project centred around Hardware-Software Co-design.

I have key hardware design and development experience. These days i am associated with the Centre for

Electronic Design and Technology (CEDT) and the Texas Instruments Centre for Embedded Product Design. I am currently a Research Assistant with Texas Instruments working on primarily TI based Cortex-M3 ARM microcontrollers.

Any expertise in hardware and system design must be complemented by knowledge of software. Having previously worked as a Product Engineer & Software Developer at SEETA (Software for Education, Entertainment and Training Activities) and as an active bug patcher at Sugarlabs, i have seen both sides of the coin. Lead developer of an Android-based Spreadsheet Application AnalytXLS.

A special mention of Dhananjay V. Gadre, Associate Professor, ECE Division, NSIT for being a great mentor and allowing unrestricted access to his labs.
