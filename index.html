Bio

I am pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and Communication from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi, India.

I'm passionate about technology, embedded systems and the Web. I love learning new technologies, particularly those that allow build robust systems and which give an interative user experience. I am an Open Source hardware and software enthusiast who firmly believes in Free Software, Free Society.

My interests include Embedded System Software and Hardware Designing (FPGAs and Microcontrollers), Compilers, Android and Web Application Development. I am into Hardware-Software Co-design these days.

I have key hardware design and development experience. These days i am associated with the Centre for Electronic Design and Technology (CEDT) and the Texas Instruments Centre for Embedded Product Design. I am currently a Research Intern with Texas Instruments working on primarily TI based Cortex-M3 ARM microcontrollers.

Any expertise in hardware and system design must be complemented by knowledge of software. Having previously worked as a Product-cum-Software Developer at SEETA (Software for Education, Entertainment and Training Activities) and as an active bug patcher at Sugarlabs, i have seen both sides of the coin. Lead developer of an Android-based Spreadsheet Application AnalytXLS.

A special mention of Dhananjay V. Gadre, Associate Professor, ECE Division, NSIT for being a great mentor and allowing unrestricted access to his labs.
