about

I am an Open Source hardware and software enthusiast, passionate about technology, embedded systems and the Web. My interests include Embedded System Software and Hardware Designing (FPGAs and Microcontrollers), Compilers, Android and Web Application Development.

I will be joining the graduate program in Embedded Systems at the University of Pennsylvania in fall 2013. I did my Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and Communication from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi, India.

I have key hardware design and development experience owing to my association with the Centre for Electronic Design and Technology (CEDT) and the Texas Instruments Centre for Embedded Product Design. Previously, I was a Product-cum-Software Developer at SEETA (Software for Education, Entertainment and Training Activities) and an active bug patcher at Sugarlabs. These days I am into technology consulting and building something of my own.

A special mention of Dhananjay V. Gadre, Associate Professor, ECE Division, NSIT for being a great mentor and allowing unrestricted access to his labs.
