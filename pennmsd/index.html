LAMP - Lyrics Assisted Music Prediction

This webpage hosts the dataset used for doing the LAMP - Lyrics Assisted Music Prediction project for CIS 519 (Introduction to Machine Learning) at the University of Pennsylvania by Shanjit Singh Jajmann, Varun S. Sridhar and Jitesh Gupta.
