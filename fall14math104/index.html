Grading Policy

Your course grade will be depend on,
  • Attendance - 10%
    You need to attend both the lectures and the recitations. In order to keep your attention, we ask you not to use your computer and phones during discussions, please do not bring calculators either as they aren't allowed in any exams. Everyone should prepare to the next lecture by watching lecture videos and by reading the lecture notes online. Each week there will be a homework assigned to the groups about the lecture videos/notes.
    Videos.
    Lecture notes .
  • Homework - 20%
    The homework will have three parts individual homework (5%), group homework (5%) and reading assignments (10%). Each individual homework shall be graded out of 5 points. The lowest score in the individual homework shall be dropped. The deadline for all group and individual homework will be Tuesdays before lecture (10:30 am). If you turn in the homework after class started you lose 1 point. If you turn in the homework on Tuesday during office hours you lose 2 points. No points will be given to homeworks handed in later. No make-up homeworks.
  • Quizzes - 10%
    There will be weekly quizzes on Mondays during recitations (see time and place above). The quizzes will be based on homework problems. The worst 2 quizzes will be dropped. There are no make-up quizzes just in extraordinary cases.
  • Midterm - 30% (15% two midterms)
    First midterm - Sept. 29, 6-8pm.
    Second midterm - Nov 3, 6-8pm. More instructions on this later.
  • Final - 30%
    Exam takes place on Friday, December 12, 2014, 9-11am. More instructions on this later.
The grade distribution for the course,
  • Top 30% - Letter Grade A
  • Next 30% - Letter Grade B
  • Next 30% - Letter Grade C
  • Bottom 10% - Letter Grade D or F
